Ryan's Favorite Movies

My Top 3 Movies of 2024

1: Alien Romulus

Great atmosphere and vibe. Throwback to 80's Sci-Fi. Saw this film in a theatre Japan while traveling with my brother. The movie was in English but with Japanese subtitles.

2: Beetlejuice Beetlejuice

Another nostaligic remake/sequel. I have always thought that Michael Keaton as Beetlejuice would only get better as he aged, and I was right! Funny moments with the classic Tim Burton style. I also saw this movie while traveling this time in Thailand. Going out to the movies in Thailand is an amazing experience. Luxury theaters and seating, refills and snacks provided by a waiter before the movie started. Definitly the most comfortable theatre I have been too.

3: Ricky Stanicky

A very funny comedy that I did not expect. John Cena steals most of the film with most of the memorable punchlines and goofs. I watched this one at home on my couch, with my GF and our pets.
